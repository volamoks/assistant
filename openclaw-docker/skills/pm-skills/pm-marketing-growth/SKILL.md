# Marketing & Growth for FinTech

This skill collection helps FinTech PMs drive growth with trust-focused messaging, compliance-aware positioning, and financial product-specific metrics.

## Included Skills

### 1. value-prop-statements — Value Proposition Statements
Craft compelling value propositions for FinTech products.

**FinTech Value Proposition Framework:**

**Template:**
```
For [target customer] who [problem/need],
our product is a [category]
that [key benefit].
Unlike [alternatives],
we [differentiator].
```

**FinTech Example:**
```
For mid-market e-commerce merchants who struggle with cart abandonment,
our product is a BNPL solution
that increases conversion by 20% through flexible payment options.
Unlike traditional financing,
we offer instant approval with no credit risk to the merchant.
```

**Trust-First Messaging:**
- Lead with security ("Bank-level encryption")
- Emphasize compliance ("Licensed and regulated")
- Show social proof ("Trusted by 10,000+ merchants")
- Transparent pricing ("No hidden fees")

```

```

### 3. north-star-metric — North Star Metric
Define the single metric that best captures product value.

**FinTech North Star Candidates:**

**Payment Products:**
- Transaction volume (GMV)
- Number of active merchants
- Payment success rate

**Lending/BNPL Products:**
- Loans originated / BNPL volume
- Active borrowers
- Repeat usage rate

**Wallet/Account Products:**
- Monthly active users
- Transaction frequency
- Assets under management

**Qualities of Good FinTech NSM:**
- Reflects customer value received
- Correlates with revenue
- Actionable by product team
- Not easily gamed

**Example NSM Selection:**
```markdown
## North Star: Weekly Active Transactors

**Definition:** Users completing at least one transaction per week

**Why This Metric:**
- Captures habit formation
- Correlates with LTV
- Actionable (onboarding, engagement features)
- Leading indicator of revenue

**Input Metrics:**
- Signup completion rate
- First transaction rate
- Weekly transaction frequency
```

## FinTech Growth Best Practices

### Trust-First Growth
- Security messaging in all campaigns
- Social proof prominently displayed
- Transparent pricing (no hidden fees)
- Clear regulatory status

### Compliance-Aware Marketing
- Review all claims with legal
- Include required disclosures
- Avoid misleading interest rate presentations
- Document consent for communications

### Metrics That Matter

**Growth Metrics:**
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- LTV (Lifetime Value)
- LTV/CAC ratio (target >3x)
- Payback period (target <12 months)

**Engagement Metrics:**
- Activation rate
- Retention (D1, D7, D30)
- Feature adoption
- NPS score

**Financial Metrics:**
- Take rate (revenue/GMV)
- Fraud rate
- Default rate (lending)
- Unit economics
