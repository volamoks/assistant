# Product Strategy for FinTech

This skill collection helps FinTech PMs develop product strategy with attention to regulatory constraints, financial unit economics, and competitive positioning.

## Included Skills

### 1. business-model — Business Model Canvas
Map your FinTech business model comprehensively.

**FinTech Business Model Canvas Adaptations:**

**Key Partners:**
- Banking partners (issuing, acquiring)
- Payment networks (Visa, Mastercard)
- KYC/AML providers
- Fraud detection services
- Regulatory consultants

**Key Activities:**
- Risk underwriting
- Compliance monitoring
- Fraud prevention
- Customer onboarding/KYC
- Payment processing

**Key Resources:**
- Regulatory licenses
- Banking relationships
- Risk models
- Customer data (compliant)
- Technology platform

**Value Propositions:**
- For merchants: Increased conversion, reduced fraud
- For consumers: Flexible payments, financial access
- For banks: Distribution, new customer segments

**Customer Relationships:**
- Self-serve onboarding
- Account management for enterprise
- 24/7 support for financial issues
- Community for developers

**Channels:**
- Platform integrations (Shopify, etc.)
- Direct sales (enterprise)
- API/developer portal
- Partner referrals

**Customer Segments:**
- Merchants (by size, vertical)
- Consumers (by creditworthiness)
- Developers/API users

**Cost Structure:**
- Cost of funds (for lending)
- Payment processing fees
- Fraud losses
- Compliance costs
- Technology infrastructure

**Revenue Streams:**
- Transaction fees (take rate)
- Interest income (lending)
- Subscription fees (SaaS)
- Interchange (card products)

### 2. pricing-strategy — Pricing Strategy
Develop pricing for FinTech products.

**FinTech Pricing Models:**

**Transaction-Based:**
- Percentage of transaction (e.g., 2.9% + $0.30)
- Per-transaction fee
- Tiered by volume

**Subscription (SaaS):**
- Monthly/annual platform fee
- Tiered by features or volume
- Freemium model

**Usage-Based:**
- API call pricing
- Storage/compute fees
- Active user pricing

**Lending/BNPL:**
- Merchant fee (typically 2-6%)
- Consumer interest (0% for BNPL, higher for loans)
- Late fees (regulated)

**FinTech Pricing Considerations:**
- **Regulatory Caps:** Some jurisdictions limit fees
- **Transparency:** Required disclosures (APR, total cost)
- **Competitive Benchmarks:** Market rate comparisons
- **Unit Economics:** Must cover cost of funds + risk

**Example Usage:**
```
Use skill: pricing-strategy
Args: BNPL product for e-commerce merchants
```

### 4. swot-analysis — SWOT Analysis
Analyze strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats.

**FinTech SWOT Factors:**

**Strengths:**
- Regulatory licenses
- Banking partnerships
- Technology platform
- Risk models

**Weaknesses:**
- Limited capital
- New brand (trust deficit)
- Regulatory complexity
- Fraud exposure

**Opportunities:**
- Open banking APIs
- Embedded finance trend
- Underserved segments
- International expansion

**Threats:**
- Regulatory changes
- Incumbent competition
- Economic downturn (credit risk)
- Cybersecurity threats

---

### 5. porters-five-forces — Competitive Analysis
Analyze industry competitive dynamics.

**FinTech Five Forces:**

**Threat of New Entrants:**
- High (low barriers for tech)
- BUT: Regulatory requirements create moats
- Capital requirements for lending

**Bargaining Power of Suppliers:**
- Banks (limited partners)
- Payment networks (oligopoly)
- KYC providers

**Bargaining Power of Buyers:**
- Merchants (can switch processors)
- Consumers (low switching costs)
- Price sensitivity

**Threat of Substitutes:**
- Traditional banks
- Other fintechs
- Cash/checks

**Competitive Rivalry:**
- Intense in popular segments
- Differentiation through features, pricing, service

## FinTech Strategy Best Practices

### Regulatory Strategy
- Obtain licenses early (long lead times)
- Build compliance into product design
- Monitor regulatory trends
- Engage with regulators proactively

### Risk Management
- Diversify funding sources
- Build robust fraud prevention
- Maintain capital reserves
- Stress test scenarios

### Partnership Strategy
- Banking partnerships are critical
- Platform integrations drive distribution
- API ecosystem for developers

### International Expansion
- Research regulatory requirements
- Local partnership needs
- Currency/FX considerations
- Cultural payment preferences
